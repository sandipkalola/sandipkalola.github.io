Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

EdgeAttend AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the EdgeAttend AI mobile application.

Information Storage

EdgeAttend AI stores attendance records, user profiles, and facial recognition data locally on the user's device.

We do not transmit, upload, sell, rent, or share this information with our servers or any third parties.

Face Data Usage

EdgeAttend AI uses facial recognition technology solely for attendance verification purposes.

Facial images and biometric templates are processed and stored only on the user's device.

Face data is never uploaded to external servers and remains under the control of the device owner.

Permissions Used

Camera

The camera is used to capture facial images for attendance verification.

Storage

Storage access may be used to save attendance records, profile images, and application-related data on the device.

Data Sharing

EdgeAttend AI does not share personal information, attendance records, facial images, or biometric data with third parties.

All information remains stored locally on the user's device unless the user explicitly exports or shares it.

Data Retention

Data remains stored on the user's device until it is deleted by the user or removed when the application is uninstalled.

We do not maintain copies of user data on external servers.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the application. However, users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.

Children's Privacy

EdgeAttend AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
